  1. Correct Dimensions and Bleed:

    - For A4 size: Set up your document size as 210 mm x 297 mm (8.27" x 11.69") with a bleed of 3 mm (0.125 inches) on all sides.

    - For A5 size: Set up your document size as 148 mm x 210 mm (5.83" x 8.27") with a bleed of 3 mm (0.125 inches) on all sides.

    - For DL size: Set up your document size as 99 mm x 210 mm (3.90" x 8.27") with a bleed of 3 mm (0.125 inches) on all sides.

  2. Convert your design to CMYK color mode for accurate color representation in print. Ensure images are high resolution (at least 300 dpi) to maintain quality.

  3. Ensure that the fold line is positioned accurately at the halfway point of the document. This is crucial for a clean fold and professional appearance.

  4. Provide the file in vector formats like PSD/Ai/PDF or CDR file formats.

  5. Embed or outline all fonts used in your design to prevent font substitution issues.

  6. Choose a suitable paper stock and finish based on your preferences and budget. Common options include matte, gloss, or uncoated finishes, with varying paper weights.
